MAPS - SUMMER MEETINGS


          Gibbons Park

Gibbons Park is a city park that winds it's way along the Thames River just south and east of campus.  To find our juggling spot follow the bike path to approximately 500 feet north of the swimming pool.  Just off the path is a small valley which provides a shady spot to juggle with some moderate protection from the wind.  Keep in mind that there is no shelter here if the weather starts to turn bad, however, so meetings are weather dependent.

          UCC - Concrete Beach

Meetings are held on the grassy area just off of the concrete beach in front of the UCC.  In case of bad weather, we can move our meetings indoors.  Parking can be found at the D B Weldon  parking lot and in the Social Science parking lot.  I don't know how parking works on campus during the summer.
