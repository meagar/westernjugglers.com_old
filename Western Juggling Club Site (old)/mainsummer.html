Summer Juggling:  Although not officially part of the Western Juggling Club activities, several of our memnbers get together in the summer to juggle in the beautiful outdoors that London has to offer.  This year we will be alternating between our usual summer meeting place, just off the bikepath in Gibbons Park, and the grassy section just off the concrete beach, in front of the UCC on campus.  See MAPS page for details.  During meetings in Gibbons Park, those of you who are comfortable using fire props may do so CAREFULLY and AT YOUR OWN RISK.  Meetings will be from 6:30 - 8 p.m. and will alternate between Sundays and Mondays, where possible.   NOTE:  There is no shelter at Gibbons Park to juggle under - i.e. if it is raining, the meeting is cancelled.  If it is raining for a Monday meeting, look for us inside the atrium of UCC.
