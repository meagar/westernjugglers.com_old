Contact the juggling club by email:
 usc.clubs.juggling@uwo.ca

 Join our mailing list:
 E-mail usc.clubs.juggling@uwo.ca with the message "Juggling News, Subscribe Me" as the subject of the e-mail. Within a few days, you will receive a confirmation message and will then be subscribed to the mailing list. We strongly suggest you sign up for this service, so you can have the most current information on meeting locations and activities, without checking this web-site.

 Stop by our meetings:
 We meet quasi-weekly to juggle together on Sunday evenings, 7-9pm. Check the calendar for dates and locations. Visitors are more than welcome!
