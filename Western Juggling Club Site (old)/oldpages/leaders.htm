There aren't any REAL club officers here (jugglers are like that), but in order to be a ratified Western club, on behalf of the University Students' Council, here are your "official" officers. In reality, we're no different from you.. and even YOU!
President

Hey there! My name is Ryan Marttala. Somehow, some very misguided people let me become of the "leader" of this outfit! (Not that I'm complaining!) Before I first joined the club, I couldn't even throw a ball, let alone catch one. It's amazing what a little practice can do! As for me, I'm an upper-year music student studying composition. I keep busy by doing this (juggling), my homework, foot patrol, choir directing, playing piano, and WUFS. I DO enjoy being busy! My fav ice cream would have to be the "Beam Me Up Scotty" Sundae. (Not kidding!)

VP Finance

Hi, I'm Peter "Pete" Cavell, the VP Finance. I'm a third-year music student, a two-time music soph, and involved with the Improv Club (WiiiA), CHRW radio, and the campus tour guide service. Throughout my tedious and exemplary childhood, I tried to teach myself to juggle many many times, with no success. I felt myself to be a failure in every possible way. But within two weeks of joining the UWO Juggling Club, I was cascading like crazy! My floor in res even voted me "Most Likely to Run Off to the Circus".
